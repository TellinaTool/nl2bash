NAME
       grub-menulst2cfg - transform legacy menu.lst into grub.cfg

SYNOPSIS
       grub-menulst2cfg [INFILE [OUTFILE]]

DESCRIPTION
SEE ALSO
       grub-mkconfig(8)

       The  full  documentation  for  grub-menulst2cfg  is  maintained  as  a Texinfo manual.  If the info and grub-
       menulst2cfg programs are properly installed at your site, the command

              info grub-menulst2cfg

       should give you access to the complete manual.
