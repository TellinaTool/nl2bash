NAME
       mgrtopbm - convert a MGR bitmap into a portable bitmap

SYNOPSIS
       mgrtopbm [mgrfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads a MGR bitmap as input.  Produces a portable bitmap as output.

SEE ALSO
       pbmtomgr(1), pbm(5)
