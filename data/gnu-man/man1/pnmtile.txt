NAME
       pnmtile - replicate a portable anymap into a specified size

SYNOPSIS
       pnmtile width height [pnmfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads  a  portable  anymap  as  input.  Replicates it until it is the specified size, and produces a portable
       anymap as output.

       pnm(5)
