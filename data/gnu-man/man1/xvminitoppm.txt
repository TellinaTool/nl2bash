NAME
       xvminitoppm - convert a XV "thumbnail" picture to PPM

SYNOPSIS
       xvminitoppm [xvminipic]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads  a  XV  "thumbnail"  picture (a miniature picture generated by the "VisualSchnauzer" browser) as input.
       Produces a portable pixmap as output.

SEE ALSO
       ppm(5), xv(1)
