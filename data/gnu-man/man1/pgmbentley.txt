NAME
       pgmbentley - Bentleyize a portable graymap

SYNOPSIS
       pgmbentley [pgmfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads a portable graymap as input.  Performs The Bentley Effect, and writes a portable graymap as output.

       The  Bentley  Effect  is  described in "Beyond Photography" by Holzmann, chapter 4, photo 4.  It's a vertical
       smearing based on brightness.

SEE ALSO
       pgmoil(1), ppmrelief(1), pgm(5)
