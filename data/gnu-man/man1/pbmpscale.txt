NAME
       pbmpscale - enlarge a portable bitmap with edge smoothing

SYNOPSIS
       pbmpscale N [ pbmfile ]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads  a  portable  bitmap  as  input, and outputs a portable bitmap enlarged N times. Enlargement is done by
       pixel replication, with some additional smoothing of corners and edges.

SEE ALSO
       pnmenlarge(1), ppmscale(1), pbm(5)

NOTES
       pbmpscale  works  best  for enlargements of 2. Enlargements greater than 2 should be done by as many enlarge‐
       ments of 2 as possible, followed by an enlargement by the remaining factor.
