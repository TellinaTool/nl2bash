NAME
       chkdupexe - find duplicate executables

SYNOPSIS
       chkdupexe

DESCRIPTION
       chkdupexe will scan the union of $PATH and a hardcoded list of common locations for binaries.  It will report
       dangling symlinks and duplicately-named binaries.
