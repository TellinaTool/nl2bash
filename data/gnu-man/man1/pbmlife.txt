NAME
       pbmlife - apply Conway's rules of Life to a portable bitmap

SYNOPSIS
       pbmlife [pbmfile]

DESCRIPTION

       ble bitmap as output.

       A white pixel in the image is interpreted as a live beastie, and a black pixel as an empty space.
