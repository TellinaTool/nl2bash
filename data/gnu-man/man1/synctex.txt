SYNOPSIS
     synctex command [options] [args]

OVERVIEW
     The Synchronization TeXnology is a new feature of recent TeX engines.  It allows to synchronize between input
     and output, which means to navigate from the source document to the typeset material and vice versa.

     Run `synctex help' to access the built-in tool documentation.

SEE ALSO
     synctex(5) tex(1)

AUTHOR
     The Synchronize TeXnology is essentially due to Jerome Laurens, with useful suggestions by some well known
     actors of the TeX world.
