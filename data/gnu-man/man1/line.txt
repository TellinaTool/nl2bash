NAME
       line - read one line

SYNOPSIS
       line

DESCRIPTION
       The  utility line copies one line (up to a newline) from standard input to standard output.  It always prints
       at least a newline and returns an exit status of 1 on EOF or read error.

SEE ALSO
       read(1)
