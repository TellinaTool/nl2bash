NAME
       pbmtoxbm - convert a portable bitmap into an X11 bitmap

SYNOPSIS
       pbmtoxbm [pbmfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads a portable bitmap as input.  Produces an X11 bitmap as output.

SEE ALSO
       pbmtox10bm(1), xbmtopbm(1), pbm(5)
