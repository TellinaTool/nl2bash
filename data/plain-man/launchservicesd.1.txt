NAME
     launchservicesd

SYNOPSIS
     launchservicesd [--server]

DESCRIPTION
     launchservicesd is used internally by MacOS X to track and coordinate
     information about the running applications on the system.

FILES
     /System/Library/CoreServices/launchservicesd

SEE ALSO
     lsappinfo(8)

HISTORY
     launchservicesd was introduced in MacOS 10.9.
